Edge cases to consider

  • No banner zone: Should posts 4+ weeks out have no banner at all?
  • Day vs night: Should "TODAY" vs "TONIGHT" depend on the event start time?
  • "THIS AFTERNOON": Worth including or overkill?
  • Character limit: Custom messages — should we cap at ~20 chars to fit the strip?
  • Post after event: What if a post is scheduled after the event date? No banner, or "LAST NIGHT" / "YESTERDAY"?
